MOBILE RESPONSIVE UPDATE

Added a phone/tablet responsive section at the bottom of css/style.css.

What it does:
- Keeps the desktop design unchanged.
- Centers the hero text on phone screens.
- Makes grids stack cleanly on iPhone/Android.
- Prevents horizontal scrolling.
- Makes forms/buttons full-width when needed.
- Hides large decorative pieces that crowd small screens.
- Keeps the mobile menu readable.

Test in Chrome:
Right-click page -> Inspect -> Toggle device toolbar -> test iPhone and Pixel sizes.
