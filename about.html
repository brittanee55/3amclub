Hi, I’m Ashlee.

What began as a simple playlist sparked by one shadowed experience I shared has grown into a living, breathing tapestry of the strange and supernatural.

Stories started finding me: confessions from those afraid to speak, hesitating with, “I don’t want to sound crazy.” Yet here, your truths are safe and your fears are honored.

This project has become my full-time passion. I cherish reading your tales, connecting with this community, and even hearing from skeptics who question the darkness.

I post short stories daily on TikTok and Instagram, and I’m crafting longer, deeper dives through YouTube videos and podcasts, learning the craft of video and audio editing as I go so your experience is as immersive as possible. Honestly, I started knowing nothing.

I believe in God, but welcome all practices, thought forms, and religions in this community. We listen, and we do not judge.

If you want to send a story, please use the submission form at the top. My DMs are a labyrinth, flooded and impossible to navigate quickly, so this is the best way to be heard.

And lastly—a whisper of thanks to Beyoncé. If you know, you know.
