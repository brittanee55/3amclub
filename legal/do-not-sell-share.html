Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information

If the website uses advertising pixels, cross-context behavioral advertising, or other tools that may be considered “selling” or “sharing” personal information under applicable privacy laws, eligible visitors may request to opt out.

How to Opt Out

Submit a request through the Data Request page and select “Opt out of sale/share.” A production website should also connect this page to cookie consent tools, advertising pixels, and global privacy control handling where required.

Important Note

Not every website is legally required to provide this link, but including a clear privacy-choice page can make the site more transparent and professional.
