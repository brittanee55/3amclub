Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how 3AM Club / Do Good, Stay Bad (“we,” “us,” or “our”) may collect, use, store, and share information when people visit the website, submit stories, sign up for updates, purchase products, create an account, or interact with community features.

This page is written as a professional starter policy and should be customized before launch with your exact business name, address, email, tools, vendors, and retention periods.

Information We May Collect

  • Contact information: name, email address, phone number, and message content.
  • Story submissions: details a visitor voluntarily submits, including whether they want to remain anonymous.
  • Account information: username, profile settings, saved journals, saved stories, and preferences if accounts are enabled.
  • Commercial information: orders, products purchased, billing details, shipping details, refunds, and customer support history.
  • Device and usage information: IP address, browser type, pages visited, referring page, approximate location, cookie identifiers, and analytics events.
  • User-generated content: comments, theories, community posts, uploads, polls, and investigation-room content.

Why We Collect Information

  • To operate the website and provide requested features.
  • To receive and review story submissions.
  • To send newsletters, updates, and club announcements when a user signs up.
  • To process purchases, shipping, returns, and customer service requests.
  • To improve the website, understand usage, and protect against abuse.
  • To personalize saved journals, workbooks, vault tools, or account features.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce website policies.

Cookies, Analytics, and Advertising

We may use necessary cookies to run the site, functional cookies to remember preferences, analytics cookies to understand traffic, and marketing cookies or pixels if advertising tools are added. Where required, non-essential cookies should not be placed until the visitor gives consent through the cookie banner.

Sharing Information

We may share information with service providers who help run the website, such as hosting providers, email newsletter platforms, analytics tools, payment processors, shipping providers, fraud-prevention services, and customer support tools. We do not intend to sell personal story submissions. If advertising pixels or targeted advertising tools are used, visitors may have additional opt-out rights.

Retention

We keep information only as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described here, unless a longer period is required by law. Example: email signup information may be kept until unsubscribe; order records may be kept for accounting and tax requirements; story submissions may be kept unless deletion is requested and no legal reason requires retention.

User Rights

Depending on location, visitors may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, portability, restriction, or opt-out of certain data uses. Use the Data Request page to submit a privacy request.
